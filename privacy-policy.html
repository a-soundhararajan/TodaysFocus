Privacy Policy for Today's Focus

Effective Date: July 2025

Important: Today's Focus is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Task titles and descriptions you create
  • Due dates and reminder settings
  • Category assignments and priority levels
  • Completion status and timestamps

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • Notification preferences and settings

Local Storage:

  • All task data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is transmitted to external servers
  • Data is encrypted using iOS security features

2. How We Use Your Information

App Functionality:

  • To provide task management and reminder services
  • To display statistics and progress tracking
  • To enable notification features
  • To maintain your preferences and settings

Improvements:

  • To analyze app performance and identify bugs
  • To improve user experience and features
  • To ensure app stability and reliability

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your personal information
  • Data is protected by iOS security measures
  • You have complete control over your data

Backup:

  • Your data may be included in iCloud backups (if enabled)
  • This is controlled by your iOS settings, not our app
  • We cannot access your iCloud data

4. Notifications

Permission:

  • We request notification permissions to send reminders
  • You can disable notifications at any time
  • Notification settings are stored locally on your device

Usage:

  • Notifications are used only for task reminders
  • We do not send promotional or marketing notifications
  • You control when and how notifications are delivered

5. Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Data Sharing:

  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising networks
  • We do not integrate with external analytics services

Apple Services:

  • We use Apple's notification services (required for reminders)
  • We use Apple's app distribution platform (App Store)
  • These services are subject to Apple's privacy policies

6. Your Rights and Choices

Data Control:

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can modify or delete individual tasks at any time
  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings
  • You can export your data through the app's export feature

Access and Correction:

  • All your data is accessible within the app
  • You can edit or delete any information you've created
  • No external access is required to manage your data

7. Children's Privacy

  • Our app is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent and believe your child has provided information, contact us

8. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time
  • Changes will be posted in the app and on our website
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes

9. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For EU Users:

  • We process your data based on legitimate interest (app functionality)
  • You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your data
  • You have the right to data portability
  • You can withdraw consent for notifications at any time
